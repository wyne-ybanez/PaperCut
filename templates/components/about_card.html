About Us

This site aims to gather reviews or rants from its users regarding any novels they have read. You can read another user's review(s) to gather more information about certain titles. You can search for specific users, check their profile and see what they have posted. You can also search for specific content through the search bar at the top or the genre list the side of our main page.
